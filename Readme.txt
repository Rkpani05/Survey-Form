Survey Form for a Developer
This is a simple survey form designed for developers to share their experience and opinions on various aspects of software development. The form has been developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Survey Questions
The survey form consists of the following questions:

Name: Enter your name.
Email: Enter your email address.
Age: Enter your age.
Current role: Select your current role from the options provided.
Best suited programming language and tools: Select the programming language and tools that you think are best suited for your work.
Rating: On a scale of 1 to 5, rate yourself as a developer.
Webinar subscription: Select whether you would like to subscribe to our weekly programming webinars.
Feedback: Provide any additional comments or feedback you would like to share.
Privacy: We assure you that we will never share your data with anyone else.
How to Use the Form
To use the survey form, simply fill out each question with the appropriate information. Once you have completed all of the questions, click the "Submit" button to submit your responses. Your responses will be sent to us and we will use them to improve our services and provide you with better content in the future.
